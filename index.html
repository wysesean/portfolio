Scribal

  Berkeley University used to host 20,000 free online educational videos. On March 11th they were sued as none of their almost 20,000 educational videos had any transcriptions which forced them to take them all down. This is where Scribal comes in, it rehosts those videos and transcribes them through a crowd-sourced transcription system that gets more accurate as time goes on.

REACT, NODE.JS, MONGODB, BACKBONE.JS, EXPRESS, SASS, VIDEO.JS, CLOUDINARY, MATERIALIZE, JQUERY.
